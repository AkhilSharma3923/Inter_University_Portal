About Us

The Government of Jharkhand is committed to promoting innovation and excellence in education. In line with this vision, we are developing an online integrated platform where all universities/colleges in Jharkhand can provide information about the projects done by their students. This platform will be a great resource for students, researchers, and industry professionals alike.

The platform will have a number of features, including:
* A searchable database of student projects from all universities/colleges in Jharkhand.
* The ability for students to upload their projects to the platform and share them with others.
* Tools for plagiarism detection and prevention.
* Forums for students to discuss their projects and collaborate with others.

We believe that this platform will play a significant role in promoting innovation and excellence in education in Jharkhand. By providing students with a platform to share their work and collaborate with others, we are helping them to develop the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in the 21st century.
